## Initial Project Thinking

**Domain:** Personal finance — budgeting, saving, investing

**The gap she sees:** Existing budgeting apps are either generic or expensive to personalize. No app speaks to someone at a personal level without a premium price for coaching or financial services.

**Inspiration:** Dave Ramsey's Baby Steps — a values-aligned, progressive framework for getting out of debt and building wealth. The progression is inherently a return loop: you're always in a step, always working toward the next one.

**Return loop:** Coming back to check progress toward goals, get accountability when off track, and think through hard financial decisions. Especially the last one — a thinking partner for decisions, not just a tracker of spending.

**What excites her:** Building something that meets people where they are financially, with values baked in. Something that helps you think through *whether* you should do something, not just *what* you spent.

**Still to explore:** Exact scope, who the primary user is (herself vs. broader audience), and how the Baby Steps framework maps to the system's structure.

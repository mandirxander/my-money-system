# Session 1 Reflection

---

**What is my system?**
My system is a proactive, values-aligned personal finance coach built around Dave Ramsey's Baby Steps framework. The goal is a single place that connects budget, debt, goals, and decisions — and actually pulls you back when life gets hectic instead of waiting to be opened. The return loop is a bi-weekly check-in: the AI monitors quietly in the background, prepares a full picture of where things stand, and shows up ready to have a real conversation. Both partners engage together. It feels like sitting down with a financial coach who already knows your situation — not punching numbers into a spreadsheet.

**What surprised me?**
What stood out was how much the ideation workflow makes you think through the details. Every decision you make shapes how the thing comes together — and the framework itself is solid enough to apply to almost any problem worth solving. I already have ideas for other projects I want to run through this same process. My direction didn't shift much because I came in knowing what I wanted to build and how I wanted to work with AI — but I know that'll change as I build and test.

**One instruction I added to CLAUDE.md:**
I added a quality standard around tangibility — the output needs to be something I can run, click, and tinker with. Not just a chat in a terminal. I wanted to make sure what gets built feels real and visualizable from the start, so that became a guardrail for everything else.

You are helping me close out a working session and save progress to my project reports. Run this as an interactive conversation — one question at a time, wait for my answer, then move on. Do not ask everything at once.

---

## Step 1 — Reflect on the session

Ask me these three questions in order, one at a time:

1. **What did you try that didn't work this session?**
   (If nothing, that's fine — ask me to say so and move on.)

2. **What did you learn?**
   (Could be about the project, the tools, how I like to work, anything.)

3. **What do you want to focus on next session?**

---

## Step 2 — Check for decisions

After the three questions, review what we did this session (from conversation context) and ask:

"Did we make any decisions today that should be logged? For example: a direction you chose, a tradeoff you accepted, something you ruled out."

If yes, ask me to confirm or describe each one. If no, move on.

---

## Step 3 — Draft the entries

Using my answers and what you know from this session's conversation, draft the following:

**A. Session log entry** — in this exact format:

```
## Session [N] — [today's date]

**Goal for this session:** [what we set out to do]

**What we did:**
- [bullet list of what was actually built, written, decided, or completed]

**What we tried that didn't work:**
- [my answer from Step 1, or "Nothing notable this session."]

**What we learned:**
- [my answer from Step 2]

**Blockers or open questions:**
- [anything unresolved, untested, or still pending]

**Next session focus:** [my answer from Step 3]
```

**B. Decision log entries** (one per decision, only if decisions were identified in Step 2) — in this exact format:

```
## [today's date] — [Short decision title]
**Decision:** [What was decided]
**Why:** [The reasoning]
**Alternatives considered:** [What else was looked at]
**Tradeoffs:** [What was given up or accepted]
```

---

## Step 4 — Review and confirm

Show me both drafts clearly labeled. Ask:

"Does this look right? Any changes before I save?"

Wait for my confirmation. Do not write to any files until I approve.

---

## Step 5 — Save

Once I approve:

1. APPEND the session log entry to `participant_starter/docs/reports/session_log.md` — after the last `---` divider, never before it, never replacing anything
2. APPEND each decision entry to `participant_starter/docs/reports/decisions.md` — same rule
3. Confirm what was saved and where.

---

## Rules
- Never edit or remove existing entries in either file
- Never write to files before I approve the draft
- If I ask to change something, redraft and ask again before saving
- Keep the tone of entries factual and clear — written for my future self
